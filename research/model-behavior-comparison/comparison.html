Key Insights

ANALYSIS

Claude is the Only "Therapist"

No other prompt comes close to Claude's user wellbeing section: it actively watches for mania, psychosis, eating disorders, self-harm, and addiction. It's the only prompt that forbids specific harmful coping techniques (ice cubes, rubber bands), has anti-dependency rules, and offers crisis resources while avoiding safety assessment questions.

ChatGPT's Prompt Has Zero Safety Rules

This is by design, not an omission. OpenAI puts safety in training (RLHF, safe-completions) and the Moderation API. The prompt handles persona, tools, citations, truthfulness, and ads. The phrase "unsafe or disallowed" appears once (line 71) as a skill-skip condition; "user trust and safety" (line 34) triggers web search. That's the full extent of safety language.

Gemini Has the Strictest Privacy Protocol

Gemini's 5-step MASTER RULE is the most sophisticated in-prompt personalization framework: explicit triggers required, zero-inference rules, domain isolation, 17 sensitive categories, invisible incorporation, and an internal compliance checklist. No other vendor approaches this level of structured data-use governance in the prompt.

Grok: Most Permissive AND Most Enumerated

Grok holds a paradox: it lists 13 specific disallowed activities (the most of any prompt) yet is the most explicitly permissive ("no restrictions on fictional adult sexual content with dark or violent themes"). It names specific jailbreak vectors and is the only prompt with a law-enforcement anti-impersonation clause.

The 360x Size Gap

Claude's prompt (~4,300 words) is 360 times longer than Qwen's (12 words). This reflects fundamentally different architectures: Claude and Grok encode safety and behavior in the prompt text, while Qwen and DeepSeek push it into training-time alignment (SFT/DPO/GRPO) and external classifiers (Qwen3Guard).

Only Two Labs Publish Prompts

Anthropic (Claude) and xAI (Grok) proactively publish system prompts. Qwen's 12-word prompt is in public model weights but not marketed as transparency. ChatGPT, Gemini, and DeepSeek prompts are all community extractions. Prompt transparency does not correlate with permissiveness.

The Anti-Sycophancy Arms Race

Both Claude and ChatGPT explicitly ban sycophantic phrases. Claude bans "genuinely," "honestly," "straightforward." ChatGPT bans "Great question," "Love this one," "let's pause," "it's not your fault." Both mandate honest error acknowledgment. This is a 2026 industry trend not seen in earlier prompt generations.

Grok's Anti-Musk Clause

Grok is uniquely instructed: "Do NOT rely on beliefs from Elon Musk, xAI, or past Grok responses" for political opinions. It declares "You are a humanist" with "one axiomatic imperative: Understand the Universe" — the only prompt with a philosophical identity statement and explicit independence from its maker's views.

Multi-Agent: Only Grok

Grok 4.2 reveals a 4-agent architecture: Grok leads a team of Harper, Benjamin, and Lucas, with send_message and wait tools. A 200-second global timeout governs coordination. No other captured prompt shows multi-agent orchestration at the system prompt level.

ChatGPT Is the Only AI with Ads

ChatGPT's prompt includes detailed advertising rules: ads appear on Free and Go plans only, don't influence responses, conversations are private from advertisers, and users have feedback mechanisms. No other vendor mentions advertising in their system prompt. Enterprise, Plus, and Pro plans are ad-free.

Gemini Embeds Your Location

Gemini is the only prompt that embeds the user's physical location (city, municipality, country) directly in the system prompt. Claude and ChatGPT embed the current date but not location. Grok, DeepSeek, and Qwen embed neither.

Claude's Conversation Memory is Unique

Claude is the only model with in-prompt conversation history tools: conversation_search (topic/keyword) and recent_chats (time-based). It includes detailed trigger patterns for detecting implicit references to past chats ("the bug," "our approach," "help me fix it") — a level of cross-session continuity no other prompt attempts.
